Key Design Decisions

Rationale behind visual direction

Dark Mode First

Design work shows best on dark backgrounds. Reduces eye strain, focuses attention on content. Muted palette lets colorful design work stand out.

Inter Font

Clean, highly readable, excellent at small sizes, open source. System fallbacks ensure fast loading and native feel.

8px Spacing System

Consistent rhythm throughout the interface. All spacing values are multiples of 8px (4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, etc.).

Card-Based Layout

Scannable at a glance, groups related information, easy to add/remove/reorder. Proven pattern from Notion and Linear.

Blue Accent

Professional, trustworthy, not overwhelming. Used sparingly for interactive elements. Neutral grays everywhere else.

Status Colors

Phase-specific colors make project status instantly recognizable. Purple (research), blue (concepts), yellow (production), green (QA).
